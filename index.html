Welcome to a sacred space where the wisdom of tarot and oracle cards illuminates your journey. At Divination Awakened, we offer personalized, heartfelt readings to help you gain clarity, find direction, and connect with your inner wisdom. Whether you're seeking answers about love, career, personal growth, or anything else, my intuitive readings provide insight and empowerment.

How it Works:

  1. Take a look at the collective readings, or pick a personal reading for more accurate details.
  2. Fill out the form to purchase a personal reading of your choosing. Tell us a few things we might need to know.
  3. Once you submit the form, you will be redirected to the personal readings page. We will send a Zelle request to the phone number listed, which you will pay for the recording.
  4. Within a week, you will receive a recording with your reading. If we go over the time selected, we will not charge more.
